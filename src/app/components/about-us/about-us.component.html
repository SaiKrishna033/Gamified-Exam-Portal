About us

Kuhedu is a 'Education-as-a-Service' (EaaS) platform that offers students in India a comprehensive collection of practice papers across various subjects, all wrapped in engaging gamification. This app not only caters to the academic curriculum but also motivates and challenges students to enhance their knowledge and skills. Kuhedu's unique value lies in its personalized and interactive learning approach, allowing students to learn at their own pace, receive instant feedback, and enjoy a gamified experience. Additionally, it harnesses the power of data analytics and AI to provide valuable insights and recommendations based on individual performance and preferences. In a rapidly growing Indian student demographic, Kuhedu stands out for its affordable and effective exam preparation, blending content, gamification, and user-friendliness. Collaborations and network effects further expand its reach, making a significant impact in the EdTech landscape.
